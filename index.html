Pancakes Recipe

Ingredients

  • 150g of flour
  • 1 tablespoon of oil
  • 1 tablespoon of sugar
  • 1 teaspoon of baking powder
  • 200 ml of milk
  • 1 egg

Cooking instructions

  1. Mix angg and sugar in a bowl
  2. Add the oil
  3. In another bowl mix flour and baking powder
  4. Mix half of the dry ingredients with the egg and sugar
  5. Slowly add milk and dry ingredients (be careful not to make lumps)
  6. Warm a pan with oil
  7. Once warm put a laddle of batter
  8. Flip when bubbles on top pop
  9. After less than a minute serve and eat warm
